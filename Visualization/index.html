Hearing Aids and Costco

One of the first to sell over-the-counter hearing aids is the popular wholesale store, Costco. Hearing aid accessibility should be vast considering its allowance of hearing impaired persons to participate in daily activities with less accomodations.

Scope of hearing loss in the U.S.

Costco has the ability to increase accessibility for the hearing impaired. With new over-the-counter product, those who couldn't afford hearing aids now can. These are the new location oppurtunities that would benefit both Costco and those in need.

Machine Learning

SMOTEENN. Over and under sample model due to having an imbalanced data set. With a recall of 0.84 and an accuracy score of 0.86, SMOTEEN was the most predictive. The precision is 100% predictive for the ones we know, but it won't predict a new location.
